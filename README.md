## Green Ghor  -- [https://green-ghor.netlify.app/]
# Description:
Green Ghor is an indoor plant marketplace where users can easily buy and sell plants. It provides a modern, user-friendly platform for plant enthusiasts to manage their collections, explore new plants, and connect with local sellers.
# Key Features:
1. Buy & Sell Plants – Seamless transactions for indoor plant lovers.
2. Collection Management – Track and organize your plants easily.
3. Modern Design – Responsive and intuitive user interface.
4. Marketplace Connectivity – Connects buyers and sellers efficiently.
# Objective:
To create a safe, convenient, and enjoyable platform for indoor plant enthusiasts to trade plants and explore a variety of options.
